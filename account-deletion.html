Nudge – Account Deletion Request

Users of Nudge can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, please contact us at:

Nudge425@gmail.com

Please include:

  • your registered email address
  • the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, we will permanently delete:

  • your account information
  • your email address
  • your tasks and app data
  • associated user records stored in our systems

Data Retention

Some data may be temporarily retained for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes for a limited period if required.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are usually processed within 7 business days.
