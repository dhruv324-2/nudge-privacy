Privacy Policy — Nudge App

Effective Date: April 20, 2026  |  Last Updated: May 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Nudge ("we," "our," or "us") is a task reminder application designed to help users manage tasks and receive timely notifications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Nudge mobile application. By using Nudge, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws, the developer acting as the Data Controller can be contacted at Nudge425@gmail.com. We process your optional email address based on your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) to provide cloud synchronization services.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Nudge mobile application and services provided within the app. It does not apply to third-party services we do not control.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

Email Address (Optional) — If you choose to sign in, your email address is collected via Google Firebase Authentication for account identification and login only.

3.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and never on our servers:

  • Task titles and descriptions
  • Reminder schedules
  • App preferences and settings
  • Cached audio preview files

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect Advertising IDs or precise location data. However, third-party services such as Google Firebase process limited technical data to maintain app security and functionality. This includes your IP address, device type, operating system version, and anonymous Firebase Installation IDs used for analytics and crash reporting. We do not use this data for tracking across other apps.

3.4 Audio Content

Nudge retrieves short audio previews using the Apple iTunes Search API. Files are temporarily stored on your device. We do not intentionally transmit personal data in these requests. Audio content remains the property of its respective rights holders.

4. How We Use Information

We use data strictly to authenticate users, provide reminders and notifications, and maintain app functionality and security. We do not sell personal data, show ads, track users across apps, or build behavioral profiles.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We only share data with:

  • Google Firebase Authentication — for login services
  • Apple iTunes Search API — for audio previews

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention & Account Deletion

Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.

How to delete your data:

  • In-App: Go to Settings > Delete Account.
  • On the Web: If you no longer have the app installed, you can request account deletion by emailing us at Nudge425@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account.

Upon receiving a deletion request, your email address and synced tasks will be permanently deleted from Firebase servers within 14 days. Local data is deleted immediately upon uninstalling the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Nudge is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. You can delete your account in-app from Settings, and remove all local data by uninstalling the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated version.
